Vision & Mission

Vision

“We, the students of Multimedia University of Kenya, recognizing the growing importance of blockchain and emerging digital technologies, establish the Blockchain Club to promote learning, ethical engagement, and responsible innovation in accordance with the university’s statutes.”

Mission

  • Promote understanding of blockchain & distributed ledgers.
  • Provide a student-led platform for learning and practical exploration.
  • Enhance technical & analytical skills through workshops, seminars, projects.
  • Encourage innovation, creativity & responsible experimentation.
  • Foster interdisciplinary collaboration across the university.

Club activities

Workshops & Labs

Hand‑on coding labs, tutorials, demo sessions on blockchain systems, smart contracts, and DApps.

Guest lectures & panels

Industry experts, researchers, and practitioners (university approved) share insights.

Innovation challenges

Hackathons, project showcases, problem‑solving sprints with an educational focus.

Student‑led research discussions, reading sessions, and collaborative learning.
